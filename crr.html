OpenFab : a maker's guild

OpenFab connects people and skills together. This is not just a workshop where makers can work. It's also a place where they can learn, share, and dream. A place where they make it with their own bare hands.


OpenFab adhère à la charte des FabLabs et est reconnu par la Benelux Fab Foundation
